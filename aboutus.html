About Pirates Tours Tom

Welcome to Pirates Tour Tom, your trusted travel partner in Hurghada, Egypt! We are passionate travel experts dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences across Egypt’s most breathtaking destinations.

From the golden deserts of Luxor to the vibrant streets of Cairo, the serene Nile cruises, and the crystal-clear waters of the Red Sea, we offer a wide range of tours and activities tailored to make your journey truly special.

Our mission is simple – to ensure every traveler leaves with incredible memories and a smile! Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural exploration, we are here to craft the perfect trip for you.



Mahmoud(Tom) - Founder

Meet Mahmoud (Tom), the visionary founder of Pirates Tour Tom, a leading travel agency based in Hurghada, Egypt. With a deep passion for exploration and years of experience in the travel industry, Tom has dedicated himself to curating exceptional tour experiences that showcase the beauty, history, and adventure of Egypt.

At Pirates Tour Tom, the focus is on creating unforgettable memories for travelers from around the world. Whether it’s a breathtaking Nile cruise, exploring the ancient wonders of Luxor and Cairo, or indulging in the pristine beaches of the Red Sea, every tour is designed with care, comfort, and adventure in mind.
